Curriculum overview

KS4 combines core subjects with option pathways. Below is the high-level structure and recommended study load per week.

Core subjects

  • English: Language and Literature (combined pathway)
  • Mathematics: Foundation or Higher tier
  • Science: Combined Trilogy or Separate Sciences

Options

  • Humanities: History or Geography
  • Languages: French, Spanish, or German
  • Creative/Tech: Computer Science, Art, Design & Tech, Music, Drama

Study load

  • Core: 6–8 hours weekly across English, Maths, Science
  • Options: 2–3 hours per chosen subject
  • Revision: Little and often; schedule daily 30–45 minute blocks

Weekly rhythm

  • Daily: 30–45 mins focused block
  • Midweek: Mixed past paper set
  • Weekend: Topic review + self‑quiz

Techniques

  • Active recall: Flashcards, closed‑book summaries
  • Spaced repetition: Revisit topics on increasing intervals
  • Exam conditions: Timed practice, mark to official schemes

Checkpoints

  • Self‑marking: Track accuracy by topic
  • Targets: Identify weak spots; plan 2× focus blocks
  • Feedback: Share with teacher, adjust plan
